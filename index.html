Understanding Hydroponics

Hydroponics is a revolutionary method of growing plants without soil, using mineral-rich nutrient solutions dissolved in water. Plant roots are supported by inert growing media like rockwool, clay pebbles, or perlite, while precisely controlled nutrients are delivered directly to the root system.

This technique enables unprecedented control over pH, temperature, nutrients, and lighting, resulting in faster growth rates, higher yields, and dramatically reduced water consumption compared to traditional agriculture. Our GreenMind system takes this proven technology and enhances it with cutting-edge AI automation and IoT monitoring.
